How Desk2Door Sourcing Works

Learn how our on-demand sourcing model saves you time and stress. We handle the shopping, checking, and packaging, delivering complete orders straight to you in Kigali.

1

Send Your Shopping List

Fill out our digital order form by uploading a photo, PDF, Excel sheet, or Word document of your shopping list. Alternatively, skip the forms and send us a quick text list or a picture of your list directly on WhatsApp.

Go to Order Form
2

Receive Your Quotation

Within a few hours, we review your list, check prices with wholesale partners in Kigali, and draft a clear, itemized quotation listing each product and price. We send this quote to you via WhatsApp or Email.

3

Confirm & Select Payment

Review the quote and confirm your order. You can ask us to adjust items (e.g., remove items you already have or upgrade specific items). Choose your preferred payment option: MTN MoMo, Bank Transfer, or Cash on Delivery.

4

We Shop For You

Once confirmed, our personal shoppers visit trusted wholesale partners across Kigali. Sourcing directly allows us to find high-quality products, utensils, cookware, and office stationery at excellent prices, passing the savings to you.

5

Custom Packing & Labeling

We organize and package the materials into a customized, durable Desk2Door box or package. Every item is inspected for quality, and we label the package with your delivery details and instructions.

6

Doorstep Kigali Delivery

We load the packages and deliver them directly to your home, office, or school gates anywhere in Kigali (Gasabo, Nyarugenge, or Kicukiro). You inspect the items and complete the payment.

The Desk2Door Advantage

We do not maintain expensive retail stores or carry stagnant inventory. Sourcing items on-demand after you order allows us to operate with low overhead costs. This efficient structure ensures we can offer premium personal shopping, procurement, verification, and doorstep delivery services in Kigali at excellent rates.
