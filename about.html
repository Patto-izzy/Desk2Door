Our Story & Mission

Simplifying Kigali Shopping for Families & Businesses

Desk2Door was founded as a premium shopping service built around convenience. We believe procuring supplies, home essentials, and custom goods should be simple, efficient, and completely stress-free.

How It Started

Every year, families and businesses in Kigali face the same chore: taking long lists of specific kitchenware, office folders, baby supplies, or stationery items, and navigating busy markets and shops to source them.

Finding everything on a teacher's list often requires visiting multiple shops, fighting traffic, and spending hours in lines. Desk2Door was created to change this process entirely.

We started with a simple idea: what if customers could outsource shopping lists completely? By acting as personal shoppers, sourcing items directly from trusted partners, and delivering custom-packed boxes to doors, we save families and businesses valuable time.

Our Efficient Model

To maintain low costs and ensure direct savings, we operate on a highly efficient structure:

  • No Inventory: We store no items, eliminating warehouse fees.
  • On-Demand Sourcing: We buy supplies only after you confirm an order.
  • Direct Courier: Our team reviews lists and personally manages packaging and deliveries.
  • Kigali Partnerships: We buy bulk from primary stationery hubs to pass savings to you.
Desk2Door Values

Our Sourcing Principles

We align our operations with parameters that matter most to our customers: quality, convenience, and affordability.

✔

Quality Sourcing

We do not compromise on paper weights, writing ink durability, or mathematical grid accuracies. We buy products from brands teachers know and trust.

✔

Full List Sourcing

Instead of sending kits with missing folders or crayons, we search markets to complete your list. We notify you immediately if specific items require alternatives.

✔

Fair Pricing

Thanks to our on-demand sourcing model, we buy supplies at wholesale discounts and add a small, transparent service fee. You get excellent prices with no retail markups.

✔

Community Focused

We are a local Rwandan sole trader business. Every delivery and support response is handled with care. We build lasting partnerships with local families, schools, and offices.
