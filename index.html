Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our sourcing, packaging, and home delivery services in Kigali.

What kind of lists can I send? ▼
Yes! You can send us school supply lists, office supply restocks, monthly home essentials, kitchenware items, custom baby/gift needs, or any miscellaneous shopping list. You can upload files (PDF, Word, Excel, or photos of handwritten lists) or type/paste your list directly on our order page.
How are order prices calculated? ▼
Our price consists of the direct market cost of the supplies (we purchase from wholesale suppliers in Kigali to get the best rates) plus a small service and delivery fee depending on the order size and category. We always provide an itemized quote so you see exactly what each item costs before confirming your order.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Since we purchase items on-demand after you order, it typically takes 24 to 48 hours from the time you approve the quotation to buy, verify, package, and deliver your items to your doorstep in Kigali.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We currently accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo), bank transfers, and cash-on-delivery in Kigali. You will receive an invoice with our official payment coordinates along with your quotation.
What if a specific item on my list is unavailable? ▼
If an item is out of stock in Kigali, we will contact you immediately via WhatsApp or phone. We will propose high-quality alternatives, adjust the quote if necessary, or omit the item according to your preference.
