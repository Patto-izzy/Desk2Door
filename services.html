Looking for Custom School Kits?

We partner directly with schools and organizations in Kigali to prepare unified packages for whole classes or specific curricula. Tell us your budget, details, and requirements, and we will package custom boxes matching your institutional needs.

Important Note for Parents: These kit levels are intended as pricing and service references. Since we operate on an on-demand model, every kit is customized exactly according to your school list. We will buy only the specific brands and items requested by your child's teachers. The final invoice will reflect the actual sourced items. The package will be delivered in our signature Desk2Door Study Tool Kit.
